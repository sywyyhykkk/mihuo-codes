## 订单类型

```mermaid
graph TB

  Order(订单)-- omsOrder -->OmsOrder(商城订单)
  Order(订单)-- bizOrder --->BizOrder(用户订单)
  OmsOrder(商城订单)-- orderType = 0 -->OmsOrder0(商品订单)
  OmsOrder(商城订单)-- orderType = 1 -->OmsOrder1(服务订单)
  OmsOrder(商城订单)-- orderType = 2 -->OmsOrder3(协作订单)
```
